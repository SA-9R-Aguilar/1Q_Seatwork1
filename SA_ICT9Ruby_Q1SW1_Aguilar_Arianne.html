✧What is chick flick?

It is a movie genre marketed towards women. It usually follows the story of a female lead or main character working towards a goal, dealing with a problem, or dealing with a love interest.


✧Why I love films under this genre:

  • I always get second-hand embarrassment during the cheesy scenes AND I LOVE IT
  • The rollercoaster of emotions when the female lead finally achieves whatever her goals are
  • The hair, the outfits, and the fashion sense is iconic
  • Most, if not all, female leads can be great rolemodels
  • These movies usually promote women empowerment and how girls should stick together instead of competing
